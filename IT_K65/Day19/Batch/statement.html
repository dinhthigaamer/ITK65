B. Lp k hoch theo khi [BATCH]
time limit per test
0.3 seconds
memory limit per test
256 megabytes
input
standard input
output
standard output

C NN cng vic cn thc hin trn mt my. Cc cng vic c nh s 1,2,...,N1,2,...,N. My x l cc cng vic ny theo tng khi. C th, cc cng vic s c phn chia vo mt hoc nhiu khi trong mi khi l mt dy cng vic c ch s lin tc. Qu trnh x l cc cng vic bt u t thi im 0. Cc khi c x l ln lt theo nguyn tc nu khi bb cha s hiu cc cng vic nh hn s hiu cc cng vic trong khi cc th khi bb s c x l trc khi cc. Cc cng vic trong mt khi c thc hin lin tc. Ngay sau khi thc hin xong tt c cc cng vic trong mt khi, my s a ra kt qu thc hin cc cng vic . C th, thi im my a ra kt qu thc hin cng vic jj l thi im my x l xong khi cha cng vic jj.

Gi SS l thi gian my chun b x l mt khi. Vi mi cng vic ii ta c h s chi ph l FiFi v thi gian TiTi thc hin cng vic. Nu mt khi gm cc cng vic x,x+1,,x+kx,x+1,,x+k v thi im bt u thc hin cc cng vic trong khi l t th thi im a ra kt qu ca cc cng vic trong khi l t+S+(Tx+Tx+1++Tx+k)t+S+(Tx+Tx+1++Tx+k). Ch rng y a ra kt qu thc hin tt c cc cng vic trong khi ti cng mt thi im. Nu thi gian a ra kt qu ca cng vic ii l OiOi th chi ph thc hin cng vic l OiFiOiFi.

V d, gi s c 5 cng vic v thi gian chun b mt khi S=1S=1, (T1,T2,T3,T4,T5)=(1,3,4,2,1)(T1,T2,T3,T4,T5)=(1,3,4,2,1) v (F1,F2,F3,F4,F5)=(3,2,3,3,4)(F1,F2,F3,F4,F5)=(3,2,3,3,4). Nu chia cc khi theo {1,2}, {3}, {4,5} th thi gian thng bo kt qu (O1,O2,O3,O4,O5)=(5,5,10,14,14)(O1,O2,O3,O4,O5)=(5,5,10,14,14) v chi ph thc hin cc cng vic l (15,10,30,42,56). Tng chi ph ca mt cch phn chia cc cng vic l tng chi ph thc hin ca tt c cc cng vic. V d, tng chi ph ca cch phn chia trn l 153.

Yu cu: Hy tnh tng chi ph ti thiu.

Input
  • Dng u tin cha s nguyn dng NN (N105N105 )
  • Dng th hai cha s nguyn SS l thi gian chun b thc hin mi khi (0S500S50)
  • Tip theo l NN dng, dng th ii m t thng tin v cng vic ii gm hai s nguyn Ti,FiTi,Fi (1Ti,Fi1001Ti,Fi100)
Output

Mt s nguyn l chi ph ti thiu tm c.
